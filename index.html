About Wattpad

Wattpad is a global platform for sharing and discovering stories. With over 90 million users, it allows people to read and publish books in various genres, creating a vibrant community of readers and writers. Wattpad is known for its diverse user-generated content and has helped launch numerous authors into mainstream publishing.
